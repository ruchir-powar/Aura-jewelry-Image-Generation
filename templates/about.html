About Aura Jewels (Andheri)

Founded on March 6, 2007 by Mr. Dineshchandra Shah, Aura Jewels began as a boutique mangalsutra studio. Today, our flagship Andheri West facility spans over 75,000 sq ft and employs more than 750 artisans, CAD/CAM engineers, gem-setting specialists, and quality-control professionals.

2007
Launch of boutique mangalsutra design studio in Andheri.
2011
Moved into 15,000 sq ft workshop; installed in-house CAD/CAM suite.
2015
Expanded to 50,000 sq ft; added laser welding and automated polishing lines.
2019
Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification and BIS hallmark accreditation.
2023
Launched “Aura Light” lightweight diamond collection.
